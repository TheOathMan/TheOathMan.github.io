EarthPi



EarthPi is a unique app designed to provide users with a detailed and interactive way to explore Earth through various map projections. With 27 distinct Earth projections, the app allows users to study and visualize the planet from multiple perspectives.

Key Features:

- 27 Earth Projections: Choose from a variety of projections.

- Smooth Projection Transitions: Switch between projections seamlessly with fluid animations.

- Time Control: Adjust the time to observe the dynamic interaction of sunlight with Earth’s surface across all projections.

- Celestial Tracking: View the real-time positions of the sun and moon based on the selected time.

- Live Wallpaper: Set any projection as a live wallpaper on Android devices.

Screenshot Functionality: Capture and save images of any projection for further use or sharing.

Future Development:
The developer is dedicated to expanding EarthPi’s capabilities, with plans to introduce additional projections, features, and improvements.
