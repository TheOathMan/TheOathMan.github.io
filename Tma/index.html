2. Description of the App

The Moon is a mobile application that renders the moon realistically, including its phases, liberations, and apogee/perigee in real-time and at any time instance. The App features a time controller for moving time continuously and a time picker for choosing specific time instances. The App can also run as a live wallpaper on Android devices with low power consumption.
